GS4 TurboTrainer

Bulk skill training buttons for GemStone IV

Install

⚡ GS4 TurboTrainer
Drag this button to your bookmarks bar

How to use

  1. If your bookmarks bar isn't showing, press Ctrl+Shift+B (or ⌘+Shift+B on Mac)
  2. Drag the button above to your bookmarks bar
  3. Log in and open the GS4 skill trainer
  4. Click ⚡ GS4 TurboTrainer in your bookmarks bar
  5. New +5, +10, -5, -10 buttons appear next to every skill
  6. Submit your training as normal when done
